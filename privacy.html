Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

TicTacToe: Aqua XO respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

Information We Collect

TicTacToe: Aqua XO does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store simple gameplay-related data locally on your device, such as:

  • game settings

  • game progress or scores
  • haptic/audio preferences

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to us or to any third party.

Third-Party Services

TicTacToe: Aqua XO does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Children’s Privacy

TicTacToe: Aqua XO does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Since the app does not collect personal information from any users, it does not collect personal information from children either.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Internet Connection

The app does not require an internet connection for its core gameplay features.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
