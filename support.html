Common Questions

The app is not working correctly. What should I do?

Please try closing and reopening the app. If the issue continues, make sure you are using the latest version of TicTacToe: Aqua XO available on the App Store.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. TicTacToe: Aqua XO does not require an internet connection for its core gameplay features.

Does the app collect personal data?

No. TicTacToe: Aqua XO does not collect, store, or share personal information. You can read more in our Privacy Policy.

How can I report a bug?

Please send an email to radu.oprea@aquaxo.cc and include:

  • your device model
  • your iOS version
  • the app version
  • a short description of the issue
  • screenshots or screen recordings, if helpful
